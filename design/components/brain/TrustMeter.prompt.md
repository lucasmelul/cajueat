**TrustMeter** — how the product makes the Trust Engine visible. A 3-bar meter plus natural-language label. `high`=leaf, `mid`=amber, `low`=clay. `low` means the Brain sees conflicting or thin signals and is being honest — never "bad restaurant".

```jsx
<TrustMeter level="high" pill />
<TrustMeter level="low" label="Datos en revisión" />
```
